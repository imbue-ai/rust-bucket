# Migration to v0.9.4

## .beads/last-touched is no longer tracked

It is now gitignored and treated as local-only state.

## Devcontainer installs br from a checksum-verified prebuilt binary

Reproducible installs with no source build.

### Steps
1. Stop tracking it: `git rm --cached .beads/last-touched`, and ensure
   `.beads/last-touched` is in `.gitignore`.
2. Rebuild your devcontainer to switch to the prebuilt `br`.
